Technology

Trusted, Tamper-Proof Devices

Pébble GO utilizes trusted wearables and tracker devices that are tamper-proof to allow anyone to verifiably attest their physical location (“proof of presence”) to the blockchain. Real world data oracles in action!

Pebble Tracker will be the first device supported by Pébble GO due to its verifiability, mobility, and decentralization features. Get Pebble Tracker now on CrowdSupply

IoTeX Blockchain

Pébble GO is a DApp running on the scalable, decentralized, and EVM-compatible IoTeX blockchain. Open-source and audited smart contracts will enable the issuance, circulation, and trading of XRC1155 (NFT) and XRC20 tokens in a fully decentralized manner. Users exclusively own their NFTs and govern the Pébble GO network.

DeFIoT Infrastructure

Pébble GO incorporates first-of-its-kind tokenomics that leverage DeFi & IoT (“DeFIoT”) components such as mimo DEX, ioTube, and ioPay to empower users to trade and stake NFTs, as well as interact peer-to-peer with other users. Don’t trust, verify – Pébble GO utilizes open-source contracts and transparent tokenomics for absolute trust.

FAQ

A

The initial version of Pébble GO will be open for public testing by June 2021 and will have basic capabilities including collecting NFTs using Pebble Tracker, trading NFTs, mining $PBG tokens, and more. The governance of Pébble GO will be discussed and refined by our initial community with an expected launch in Q3 2021, allowing anyone to submit governance proposals and craft the future operating rules of the Pébble GO network.

A

Pébble GO NFTs can be collected by anyone with a supported device (e.g., Pebble Tracker) that visits a specific location at a specific time for “proof of presence”. Anyone can propose new geo-fence locations and corresponding NFTs by burning $PBG, with approval from the Pébble GO governance committee during the bootstrapping phase. Over time, we will seek to fully decentralize the listing of new locations and issuance of new NFTs.

A

$PBG token is a fungible token (XRC20) on the IoTeX Network that can be openly traded on mimo DEX. $PBG can be earned by any users that obtain and stake a Pébble GO NFT with more higher $PBG rewards in early phases. $PBG holders can burn $PBG in order to add new geo-fenced locations to the global Pébble GO map, making the total supply of $PBG deflationary over time. We are planning for a fair launch of $PBG that will reward early adopters, Pebble Tracker owners, and IoTeX community members! Stay tuned for more details on $PBG by following our official channels.

A

Pebble Tracker is a “Powered by IoTeX” device that is equipped with a tamper-proof secure element (TEE), as well as GPS, climate, motion, and light sensors. Pebble Tracker writes verifiable sensor readings, such as GPS location, to the IoTeX blockchain for use in various DApps like Pébble GO. You can order Pebble Tracker on CrowdSupply. $PBG will be airdropped to Pebble Tracker holders before the initial launch of Pébble GO!
