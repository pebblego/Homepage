Technology

Trusted Wearable Device

PébbleGo uses trusted wearable devices to allow anyone to attest to the blockchain of his or her being at a specific location at a particular time with all contextual information.

Pébble Tracker will be the first such device thanks to its verifiability and interoperability with the IoTeX blockchain, yet it is portable and mobile.

IoTeX blockchain

PébbleGo is a DApp running on top of a high performant, decentralized and EVM-compatible blockchain protocol. The IoTeX blockchain has launched for 2 years, and has been running by 100+ delegates globally to process over 10 million txs. The smart contract on this blockchain enables the issue, circulation and trade of ERC1155 (NFT) tokens as well ERC20 tokens in a fully decentralized way. You own your NFT cards completely! You govern how PébbleGo would evolve!

DeFi Infrastructure

PébbleGo’s token economic leverages various DeFi components such as DEX , ioTube and ioPay to interact with users and the blockchain. Of course, we built new open-source contracts for the underlying crypto economics.
